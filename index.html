Biography

I am a joint Ph.D. student at Harbin Institute of Technology and City University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Xianming Liu and Asst. Prof. Haoliang Li. I received my B.E. from Dalian University of Technology in 2023. My research interest is trustworthy machine learning, especially learning with imperfect data.
